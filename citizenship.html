Citizenship

and how to be a leader

i believe compassion respect responsibility honesty and courage are strong components of good citizenship. Citizenship the position or status of being a citizen of a particular country. Courage the ability to do something that frightens one. responsibility the state or fact of being accountable or to blame for something. respect a feeling of deep admiration for someone or something elicited by their abilities, qualities, or achievements. Compassion sympathetic pity and concern for the sufferings or misfortunes of others. Honesty The quality of being honest. accountability is a major component of citizenship in my personal opinion, because we are responsible for our actions.
